7 Reasons NOT to Be My Boss

This arrangement isn't for everyone. Actually, it's not for most people, even most people who follow my work. Here are several compelling reasons you should not, under any circumstance, be my boss.

In all sincerity, and with kindness and compassion, do not be my boss if...

  1. The dollars you spend employing me will make it difficult for you to make ends meet.
    Don't financially stretch yourself to pay me. Please. This will make me incredibly sad, which is likely the opposite of what you're trying to accomplish.
  2. You're expecting special access.
    I’ll share regular updates about what I’m working on, what my plans are, and what’s falling apart.
  3. Weigh in via polls and ideas.
    Let me know what you think I should be working on, and how I might be able to make you proud. Will I always follow direction? No. But…
  4. Fire me or give me a raise.
    Am I not up to your caliber? Lay me off. Am I outperforming your expectations? Give me a raise. My salary is in your hands.
